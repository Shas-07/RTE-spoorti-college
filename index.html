Our Story

Building Tomorrow's
Doctors, Engineers
& Officers

Spoorti PU College, under the banner of the Rural Technical Education Society (RTE), was established with a singular mission — to bring world-class integrated PU education to rural Karnataka. Situated on the prestigious REC campus at Hulkoti, we blend strong academic foundations with competitive exam mastery.
